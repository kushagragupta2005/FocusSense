Design

Human-Centered Principles

Built on core HCI principles to support rather than surveil.

01

Visibility of System Status

Color-coded badges and real-time scores keep you informed of your current state without interrupting flow. Deep Focus → Moderate → Distracted, instantly visible.

02

User Control & Freedom

Pause, resume, and end sessions at any time. All settings are configurable. No lock-in, no dark patterns. You own your attention data completely.

03

Ethical & Transparent Design

No behavioral manipulation, no engagement loops. FocusSense exists to help you focus better, not to keep you on-screen longer. Privacy by architecture.
